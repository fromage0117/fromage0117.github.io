Gilles' Australian Garlic

A usual, Gilles will be selling loose and plaited garlic at the Northside Produce Market for both of the November and December markets. The purple garlic is looking very good at this stage but unfortunately we lost our entire crop of white garlic due to flooding.

Get in touch for info and orders.

Markets

The markets are on the first and third weekends of the month, starting at 8:00 and ending at 12:00.

Where

Ted Mack Park, 200 Miller Street, North Sydney

When

5 November: We will have fresh garlic and garlic scapes. This is a once a year opportunity to enjoy these products. The garlic is just formed into cloves and is amazingly juicy and crunchy, with a milder garlic flavour. It is less pungent than mature garlic. The scapes are the inner stalk of the garlic. They are popular in Asian stir fries, and may be prepared in a stir fry, raw in a salad or cooked as you would cook asparagus. We will only have them at this market.

19 November: We will have fresh garlic at this market. The cloves are now fully formed while the garlic remains juicy and crunchy. It is more pungent. You can only buy garlic as fresh as this from a grower as the garlic is still fragile, not being fully dried. You can keep it and dry it but it is so delicious fresh!

3 and 17 December: We will have plaits of all the usual sizes: $15, $25, $40, $50 and a few at $100.
